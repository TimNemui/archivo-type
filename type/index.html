Welcome to the Archivo

The name for this type is Archivo,it was designed by a collective typefoundry based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Omnibus-Type Team, and specificly by the senior type designer Héctor Gatti. Archivo is a grotesque sans serif typeface, derived from its sibling, Chivo which was design with elegance and practicality in mind. Archivo retain the same quality but also added the technical and aesthetic characteristics for high performance typography.Archivo be used simultaneously in print and online platforms and supports over 200 world languages.
